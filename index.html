Specialities

Alterion Consulting Ltd is an independent consultancy company offering advice on all aspects of drug discovery research, development, partnering, licensing and strategy, particularly for the optimal targeting of ion channels as therapeutics. Specialities include:

  • Providing strategic, scientific and/or general advice to company management including C-level roles
  • Evaluation or development of R&D platform and individual program strategies for the identification of next generation therapeutics
  • Development of investor or partner facing materials for companies seeking venture capital investment, non-dilutive grant opportunities or asset or collaboration based deals
  • Preclinical pharmacology contributions to regulatory submissions and market access campaigns supporting the approval and launch of new medicines in the EU and USA
  • Due diligence and the appraisal of in-licensing opportunities
  • Business development activities to support new business generation and forge new partnerships
  • Identification and evaluation of potential program acquisition targets
  • Drug re-purposing and new indication evaluation and strategy
  • Advisory Boards for the critique of R&D therapeutic area or program strategy
  • Understanding, assessing and communicating scientific evidence for drug mechanism of action
  • Scientific writing, presentation and multimedia projects
