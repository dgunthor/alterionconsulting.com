Alterion Consulting Ltd is an independent company providing advice on scientific research, drug discovery, management and strategy to various clients in the Life Sciences and Healthcare industry, particularly for the optimal targeting of ion channels as therapeutics.

Typical clients include: Biotech, Pharma, Venture Capital and other investors and funding bodies, Start-ups, Academics, Management Consultancy, Contract Research Organisations (CRO) and Medical Communication Agencies.

expertise

Specialities include:

  • Providing scientific and strategic advice to company management including C-level / interim roles such as Chief Scientific Officer
  • Evaluation or implementation of Research & Development (R&D) drug discovery platform and program strategies for the identification of next generation therapeutics
  • Evaluation of novel targets, therapeutic areas and competitive landscape
  • Development of investor- or partner-facing materials in support of venture capital investment, non-dilutive grant opportunities or asset- or collaboration-based deals
  • Due diligence and the appraisal of in-licensing or out-licensing opportunities
  • Preclinical pharmacology contributions to regulatory submissions and market access campaigns supporting the approval and launch of new medicines in the EU and USA
  • Business development activities to support new business generation and forge new partnerships with companies in the Life Sciences sector
  • Advisory Boards for the critique of R&D therapeutic area or program strategy
  • Understanding, assessing and communicating scientific evidence for drug mechanism of action
  • Scientific writing, presentation and multimedia projects
